About

A hacker is someone who has advanced skills in computers and networks, and uses that knowledge to explore, access, or modify computer systems in unauthorized ways. The term often has a negative connotation, but this is not always the case. &

  • White Hat (White Hacker): They are hackers who use their skills for good purposes, such as identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities.
  • Black Hat (Black Hacker): They use their skills for malicious or illegal purposes, such as stealing data, damaging systems, or spreading malware.
  • Gray Hat (Gray Hacker): They are somewhere between white hat and black hat. They may exploit vulnerabilities without permission, but not with malicious intent or for personal gain.
  • Script Kiddie: This is a term for individuals who lack knowledge but use tools or scripts created by other hackers to attack systems.
